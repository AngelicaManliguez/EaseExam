EaseExam

EaseExam is a platform designed to simplify the creation of exam questions from lecture materials. It allows professors to upload files and use advanced algorithms to generate questions based on key concepts and information. The platform also incorporates artificial intelligence technology, allowing users to interact with the system via natural language commands. This allows professors to focus on teaching and facilitating learning experiences for their students, revolutionizing exam preparation.
